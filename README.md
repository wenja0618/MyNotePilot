# MyNotePilot

Have you ever had 50-page lecture slides that mentioned the assignment in the middle, and missed the deadline? Or have an exam or meeting to study for and 20 different documents to review? Meet MyNotePilot, your all-in-one review buddy. Our web app summarizes a document or folder of documents of any format (PDFs, presentations),  at your specified depth, with “very concise,” “short,” and “full” being choices. 

Furthermore, our web app connects to Google Calendar, telling you all the important events and tasks mentioned in the document, whether that be homework due dates, meeting times, or exams. The web app also lists out the events that conflict with the events from the document, giving you the option to select which events you want to add to your calendar. For tasks, you can assign hours for completing the task a week before the due date. 

Unlike passive readers, MyNotePilot really bridges the gap between information and action. It identifies dates, homework, and exam times, and pushes them directly to your Google Calendar with your approval. With smart time blocking and summarizing, the web app doesn’t just tell you what to learn, it reminds you when to do it.
